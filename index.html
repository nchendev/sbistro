Welcome To Szechuan Bistro

We are proud to serve

Is there anything better than authentic Chinese comfort food? Soft dumplings, pillowy pork buns, and fresh stir fry vegetables are enough to make any day a great day, and at Szechuan Bistro, we do all that and more. Perfect for anyone looking for a wholesome meal, our new menu boasts a variety of different options that'll satisfy any diet and palate. Everything we serve is made from scratch, and we pride ourselves in only using the freshest ingredients, resulting in food so good, you'll finish every bite no matter how full you are.

Our cozy bistro welcomes you in for a casual dining experience, but we also offer takeout if you'd rather eat your favorite dish in the comfort of your own home. We're proud to serve you in any way we can, and we know that we'll be seeing you again soon.
